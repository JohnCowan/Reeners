About Cornerstone Properties LLC
Cortland College Housing

 

Locally and Family Owned

Cornerstone Properties (CortlandCollegeHousing.com) is a family owned and operated provider of off-campus student housing for college students in Cortland, NY. We offer newly constructed luxury college student apartments and student homes in the SUNY Cortland area. "The Suites", with locations at 112 Groton Ave, 50 and 52 Tompkins St., 7 Woodruff St. and 91 Lincoln Ave are the area's only all-inclusive private college housing.

Professional and Experienced

Cornerstone Properties has been in the home building industry for over 35 years. We build to the highest quality standards in the industry with superior craftsmanship and attention to detail. "The Suites" have been designed with today's college student in mind. Providing for all modern conveniences, safety and security.

Cortland Rental Housing Law and Occupancy Law

Our buildings and apartments are in full compliance with the City of Cortland Rental Housing Law and Occupancy Law. The Occupancy Law, is a law in the City of Cortland that forbids any more than three unrelated people from living together in a house or apartment. For more information on either of these City Laws please contact the City of Cortland Code Enforcement Office at 607-753-1741.
