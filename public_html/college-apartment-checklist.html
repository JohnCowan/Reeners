Checklist of Items needed for your college apartment

 

Provided by Cornerstone Properties

Cornerstone Properties (CortlandCollegeHousing.com) takes pride in providing you with Cortland's most complete student rental package! All of our Cortland apartments are fully furnished and have 3 bedrooms, a bathroom, kitchen, dining room and living. They each include: In-Floor Radiant Heat, Hot Water, Electric, High Definition Cable, High Speed Wifi (200 Mbs Per Apartment), Free Parking, Convenient Laundry Facilities in each building right outside your apartment door. We also provide free Snow Removal and On Demand Maintanance Service.

In addition, all apartments come supplied with Miniblinds on Windows, High Speed Internet Modem, wifi Router, HD Cable Box, Shower Curtain and liner, toilet brush and plunger. Apartment light bulbs and smoke detector batteries are changed with new ones every semester and anytime they go out (stop working)!

What you will need to provide

There are several items we do not provide that you will need to for your new college apartment. Below is a list of the common items. You can bring these items with you from home or purchase them locally in Cortland. Cortland has a variety of retail stores that are a short drive from your apartment including: Super Walmart, Lowes, Big Lots, Value Home, Builders Best, Marshalls, and various dollar stores.

**** Note **** You may want to contact your roommates and divide up common area items to save money!
