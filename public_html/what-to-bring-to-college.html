Items you may need for your apartment

 

Provided by Cornerstone Properties

Cornerstone Properties (CortlandCollegeHousing.com) takes pride in providing you with Cortland's most complete student rental package! All of our Cortland apartments are fully furnished and have 3 bedrooms. They each include: In-Floor Radiant Heat, Hot Water, Electric, High Definition Cable, High Speed Wifi (200 Mbs Per Apartment), Free Parking, Convenient Laundry Facilities in each building, Snow Removal and On Demand Maintanance Service.

In addition, all apartments come supplied with Miniblinds on Windows, High Speed Internet Modem, wifi Router, HD Cable Box, Shower Curtain, liner, toilet brush and plunger. Apartment light bulbs and smoke detector batteries are changed with new ones every semester!

What you will need to provide

There are several items we do not provide that you will need to for your new apartment. Below is a list of the common things that most people need in their apartment. You may find you need other things not listed below. You can bring these items with you from home or purchase them locally here in Cortland. There is a Super Walmart here in Cortland, as well as a few Do It Yourself stores like: Lowes, Value-Home, Builders Best Do It Center, and several dollar type stores: Dollar Store, Dollar General, Family Dollar, etc.

Note: If you know who your apartment mates are, you might wish to contact them to see if you can save money by dividing up the common items in the lists.
